\section{Expressions}

Reduction of expressions on integers can be done with the following rules:
$$
{{{k} = {i + j}}\over{{i+j}\longrightarrow{k}}}
$$
$$
{{{k} = {i - j}}\over{{i-j}\longrightarrow{k}}}
$$
$$
{{{k} = {i * j}}\over{{i*j}\longrightarrow{k}}}
$$
$$
{{{k} = {i / j}}\over{{i/j}\longrightarrow{k}}}
$$
